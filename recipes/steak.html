Steak Recipe

Description

A steak recipe that takes 5 minutes to prepare, 10 minutes to cook, and holds 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 4 jalapeno peppers, stemmed
  • 4 cloves garlic peeled
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cracked black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 pounds top sirlion steak

Directions

  1. Combine jalapenos, garlic, pepper, ssalt, lime juice and oregano in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place steak in a shallow pan or large resealable plastic bag. Pour jalapeno marinade over the steak, and turn to coat. Cover pan or seal bag; marinate in the refrigerator 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grill gate.
  4. Drain and discard marinade. Grill steak 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
